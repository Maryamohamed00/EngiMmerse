Our Story

EngiMmerse was born out of our own struggles as engineering students. We, Maryam and Maya, noticed how difficult it was to find organized resources for our courses, especially when preparing for exams or working on projects.

Our mission is to simplify the academic journey for engineering students by providing a centralized platform with carefully curated resources, study materials, and guidance for various engineering majors.
